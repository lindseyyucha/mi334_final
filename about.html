About Me

My journey into the world of gaming began in childhood, and today, I'm deeply immersed in exploring game creation and design. In addition to my studies, I'm a UI programmer at Spartasoft Studio, contributing to our latest game, Market Mayhem. I bring experience from working at the Michigan State University Tech Store and interning as a journalist at the Detroit Metro Times. Whether crafting virtual adventures, solving tech dilemmas, or uncovering stories in Detroit, I'm always up for exciting challenges. Let's connect and share our passion for games and all things tech!
